The Story Of Bajaj


The Bajaj Group, an Indian multinational conglomerate, was founded by Jamnalal Bajaj in 1926. Initially focusing on cotton ginning and pressing, it diversified into sugar, textile, and eventually automobiles with the establishment of Bajaj Auto in 1945. The group continued to expand into various sectors, including finance, home appliances, and more, becoming a diversified business house.
